OpenAI was founded in December 2015 by Sam Altman, Elon Musk, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, John Schulman, and Wojciech Zaremba. The company is based in San Francisco, California.

Google was founded in September 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin while they were PhD students at Stanford University. Google's parent company is Alphabet Inc.

Microsoft was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen on April 4, 1975. The company is headquartered in Redmond, Washington. Satya Nadella is the current CEO of Microsoft.

Meta (formerly Facebook) was founded by Mark Zuckerberg, Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes in 2004. The company is headquartered in Menlo Park, California.

Amazon was founded by Jeff Bezos in July 1994 in Bellevue, Washington. Andy Jassy is the current CEO of Amazon. The company operates in e-commerce, cloud computing (AWS), and digital streaming.

Apple Inc. was founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in April 1976. Tim Cook is the current CEO. Apple is headquartered in Cupertino, California.

Tesla was founded in July 2003 by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning. Elon Musk joined as chairman and later became CEO. Tesla is headquartered in Austin, Texas.

NVIDIA was founded in April 1993 by Jensen Huang, Chris Malachowsky, and Curtis Priem. Jensen Huang has been the CEO since the company's founding.
